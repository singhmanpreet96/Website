Programming

All throughout highschool, I really didn't know the career path I wanted to go down. I was scared, didn't know what was right or wrong. Everyone had their opinions, and I just didn't know. I took numerous science classes, with the possibility of heading into the medical field. Then finally, junior year came around. A new class was being offered, AP Computer Science. I thought, "Why not? Wouldn't hurt." That was one of the best decisions of my life. I enjoyed the thrill of solving programs, the struggles of finding a solution, and harnessing the power of technology.

Cars

Cars, to most people they are simply a means of getting from point A to point B. In my case, its different. I enjoy admiring the sounds the engine and exhausts make. I drool over the sight of a properly built car. Most of all, I admire those who go out and track their vehicles. There is so much precision involved in driving at fast speeds, and I admire those who do it flawlessly. Something about seeing an Aventador whizz by just gives me the chills.
